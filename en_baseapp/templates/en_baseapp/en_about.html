RUTERM is one of the leaders in the market of steel panel radiators in Russia, Europe and the CIS, and has a well-developed distribution in this territory. The company's production complex includes a full cycle of radiator production, starting with metal dissolution, stamping, welding, painting and ending with packaging in pallets.

Our technical staff team is trained under the guidance of European specialists with unique long-term experience in world-class production facilities.
